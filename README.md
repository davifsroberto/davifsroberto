### 👋 About me

Full-stack Software Engineer with 15+ years building and evolving web products — from fintech and telecom to mobility platforms. I like staying close to the code: understanding the problem, proposing the solution, and following it through to production.

Day to day I work across **Angular, React, TypeScript, Node.js and NestJS**, with a growing focus on **Microfrontends, Design Systems and Backend for Frontend (BFF)** architecture. Lately I've been leaning into **AI-first development** — using AI agents and tooling to speed up implementation, refactors, tests and docs, without losing engineering judgment along the way.

> AI is a productivity multiplier — never a substitute for critical thinking and good engineering decisions.

- 🔭 Principal Software Engineer @ **HSystem** — full-stack, frontend architecture & technical leadership
- 🎓 Studying Computer Science, building side projects for the dev community along the way
- 💬 Happy to talk Angular, React, Microfrontends, Design Systems or AI-assisted engineering
- 📫 Find me on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/davifsroberto)
